About Me

My name is Alex Magana, and I am a Sport Management student at High Point University with a strong interest in event operations, game day management, and the behind-the-scenes work that makes sporting events run smoothly. I currently serve as a Sports Operations Manager for High Point University Club Sports, where I help coordinate logistics, scheduling, and game day operations for multiple teams across the program. This role has given me hands-on experience managing events, working with athletes and staff, and supporting in-game production elements like video boards and announcements.

Originally from Kernersville, North Carolina, I have always been drawn to leadership and service. Earning the rank of Eagle Scout and completing more than 2,000 hours of community service helped shape my work ethic and commitment to teamwork. Whether I am helping run a sporting event, organizing operations behind the scenes, or collaborating with a team to solve problems, I enjoy being part of the work that creates memorable experiences in sports.
